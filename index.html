    Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

    Progressive enhancement is like building a strong base for a house first, making sure it's solid and safe for everyone to use. Then, you add fancy decorations for those who can appreciate them without forgetting about the basic structure. It's about making sure everyone can access your website or app, no matter what kind of device they're using, and then making it even better for those with newer technology. It's a smart way to make sure your stuff works well now and in the future.
